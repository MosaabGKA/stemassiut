What alumni say

Ahmed Ibraheem

First of 2019 senior class, Math Branch

Physics major at the Faculty of Science, Assiut University

STEM is the best experience one could live in a high school. It gives its students the opportunity to grow up much faster and healthier; in an environment designed for those who want to change their micro-environment -or even the macro-environment- to the best. But it's not fair not to mention that STEM is very selective, it selects and nourishes those who are ready to give 99.99% of their best to change and lets down those who want to take it easy. So, inch your way through and give your best, and hard work will definitely pay off.
